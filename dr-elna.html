INTRODUCING
DR ELNA Rudolph

Meet our expert on intimate health and education who answers all the feminine hygiene questions you’re too shy to ask.

Dr Elna Rudolph

Dr Elna Rudolph is a full-time sexual health practitioner, and one of only two medical doctors in South Africa with a postgraduate degree in sexual health. She is also the first person with a Higher Diploma in Sexual Health and HIV Medicine through the Colleges of Medicine in South Africa, and has extensive international training and practical experience in the field of sexual health.

We’ve partnered with Dr Elna Rudolph to educate and empower women on intimate health issues. Femagene is willing to offer this dynamic individual should you have an event with the appropriate audience.

While Dr Rudolph may advise women to use Femagene products, her main purpose is to educate women on their intimate health and not to talk about Femagene specifically. Visit www.mysexualhealth.co.za for more.
