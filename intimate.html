INTIMATE HEALTH WORKSHOPS

We’re on a mission to educate women on intimate health in partnership with Dr. Elna Rudolph.

Dr. Elna is a full-time sexual health practitioner, and one of only two medical doctors in South Africa with a postgraduate degree in sexual health. She will be able to participate at your event or meeting, should there be a good match between our Femagene target market and your group of delegates. While Dr Rudolph may advise women to try Femagene products, her main purpose is to educate women on their intimate health.

We are also able to offer goodie bag inserts, giveaway hampers and informative brochures covering Femagene products. If you are considering making Femagene part of your upcoming event or conference, we would love to hear from you.
